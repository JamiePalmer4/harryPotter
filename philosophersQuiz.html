The Philosophers Quiz

Have you got what it takes to complete The Philosophers Quiz? This quiz is based on events which occurred in the movie and will test your knowledge on whether you watched the movie or not. There will be 10 questions in the quiz with no second try on a question. Upon completion of the quiz you will be shown your final score, seeing how you compare against other users who have took part in the quiz, with all results being shown on a leaderboard. Good Luck, you may need it!
